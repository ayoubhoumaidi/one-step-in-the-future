About Us

One Step in the Future is a school-certified platform presented by Groupe Scolaire Rahmouni Potentiel, where senior students conduct and publish real research on future-oriented STEM topics. The website provides a first step for students to explore their intended fields of study while gaining academic recognition.

Our Goal

Our mission is to empower students to explore and contribute to the future of STEM through hands-on research and innovation.

"No matter what your dreams or ambitions are, here you can finally take a step into them."
